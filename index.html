Welcome to Onigiring Pinoy!

Onigiring Pinoy is a homegrown food business that brings together the heart of Filipino flavors and the tradition of Japanese onigiri. We introduce a fresh twist by infusing this classic rice snack with well-loved Filipino dishes.

Our Concept

The word onigiri comes from the Japanese word nigiru, meaning “to hold.” Traditionally shaped into a triangle or ball, it is designed for easy handling and mess-free eating. At Onigiring Pinoy, we keep this convenient format while reinventing the fillings to reflect Filipino taste and culture.

Each piece is made with premium Japanese rice, lightly seasoned, generously filled, wrapped in nori seaweed, topped with furikake flakes, and sealed to maintain freshness and shape.
