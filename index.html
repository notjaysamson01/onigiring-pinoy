Welcome to Onigiring Pinoy!

Background of onigiring pinoy

Onigiring Pinoy is a tasty fusion spin on the traditional Japanese onigiri rice ball by incorporating popular Filipino comfort foods such as Tinapang Galunggong, Adobo Flakes, and Sweet Longganisa. It is a triangular-shaped rice snack designed specifically for students and busy professionals who require a convenient, affordable, and on-the-go meal. By integrating Filipino flavors with Japanese rice, seasonings, and nori, we are able to close the distance between two amazing food cultures in every bite. It’s more than just a quick bite; it’s how we can bring home to anyone, anywhere. Whether it’s a rush to class or the end of a long day, our onigiri is designed to be the perfect, mess-free companion for your busy day.

Our Concept

The word onigiri comes from the Japanese word nigiru, meaning “to hold.” Traditionally shaped into a triangle or ball, it is designed for easy handling and mess-free eating. At Onigiring Pinoy, we keep this convenient format while reinventing the fillings to reflect Filipino taste and culture. Each piece is made with premium Japanese rice, lightly seasoned, generously filled, wrapped in nori seaweed, topped with furikake flakes, and sealed to maintain freshness and shape.
